AutoNick Usage!

autonick [GR]-{/user}
AutoNick comes with a range of keys
{/user} - prints users name
{random} - random nickname
{joined} - join date
AutoNick can detect and remove special characters
and than give a random nick with below:
{special:rand}
or just remove the special characters with nothing!
{special}
